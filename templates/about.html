About Super Brick Breaker Deluxe

Game Description

Super Brick Breaker Deluxe is a modern take on the classic brick breaker arcade game. Control a paddle to bounce a ball and break bricks, collecting powerups along the way. Progress through increasingly challenging levels and aim for a high score!

How to Play

  • Use the mouse or arrow keys to move the paddle
  • Press space to launch the ball
  • Break all bricks to complete the level
  • Don't let the ball fall!
  • Collect powerups for special abilities
